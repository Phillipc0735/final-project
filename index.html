Effects Of Pollution

And ways YOU can help the environment!

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the act of dumping waste products into public spaces.
Another form of pollution is the toxins factories produce as a side effect of manufacturing.
Pollution is very harmful to the environment and, in turn, harmful to humanity.

Effects of Pollution

The effects of pollution began when humanity hit the industrial
age and began to constructfactories to produce goods to meet
the demand of the public. The factories would produce smoke
and contaminate the land around it. The factories were eventually
replaced by more environmentally safe lines of production, but
companies continue to strip the land of its resources to meet public
demand. Companies that are in logging will cut down and burn any
trees they do not need. The deforestation effects the world on a
grand scale where the effects could have catastrophic reprecussions
in the future.

Ways YOU Can Help The Environment!

There are many ways you can help the environment. You can practice
recycling your plastics and other items. You can help pick up trash in
parks and the streets to keep it clean. You can also join some
organizations to help protest the deforestation of the forests and
its effects.
